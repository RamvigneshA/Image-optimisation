Image Formats Comparison

Comparing PNG vs JPEG vs WebP vs AVIF for the exact same image.

DevTools Experiment:
  1. Open DevTools and go to the Network tab.
  2. Filter by "Img". Complete a hard reload (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R).
  3. Look at the Size column and compare the payload sizes for the different formats.

PNG

Lossless, but usually huge file sizes for photos.

Typical size: Largest (~1MB+)

JPEG

The old standard. Lossy compression, good for photos but lacks modern efficiency.

Typical size: Medium (~150KB)

WebP

Modern format, supports transparency. Usually 25-35% smaller than JPEG.

Typical size: Small (~100KB)

AVIF

Next-gen format. Superior compression. Can be 20-50% smaller than WebP!

Typical size: Smallest (~70KB)
